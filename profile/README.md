## Hi there 👋 we are Swiss Cobotics Competence Center (S3C)

The **Swiss Cobotics Competence Center (S3C)** is a national hub that supports Swiss industry in adopting **collaborative robotics**.  
Based at Switzerland Innovation Park Biel/Bienne, S3C offers a vendor-neutral environment to explore, test, and validate cobotic solutions.

## 🙋‍♀️ Mission
- Accelerate cobotics adoption in Swiss manufacturing  
- Provide practical testing and demonstration infrastructure  
- Bridge research and real-world industrial needs  
- Support SMEs with training, evaluation, and integration expertise  

## 🌈 What We Offer
- **Cobotic Base Cells (CBCs)** for safe, modular hands-on testing  
- Demonstrators for precision, medtech, lab automation, and machinery  
- Expert guidance from concept to implementation
